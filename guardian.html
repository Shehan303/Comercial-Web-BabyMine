Guardian Network

Connect your trusted circle with your child's care information

Stay Connected with Your Care Network

The Guardian Network allows you to securely share important child care information with family members, babysitters, and other trusted caregivers.

  • Real-time updates for guardians
  • Customizable access levels
  • Emergency information sharing
  • Activity tracking and logs

Guardian Network Features

Powerful tools to keep your care circle informed and connected

Multi-User Access

Add grandparents, relatives, or trusted friends with customizable permission levels.

Smart Alerts

Automatically notify guardians about important events or emergencies.

Location Sharing

Share your child's location with guardians when needed.

Medical Information

Provide instant access to allergies, medications, and doctor contacts.

Shared Calendar

Coordinate care schedules with all guardians in one place.

Activity Logs

See who accessed information and when for complete transparency.

How The Guardian Network Works

1

Invite Guardians

Send email invitations to family members or caregivers you trust.

2

Set Permissions

Choose what information each guardian can access and update.

3

Share Information

Provide access to schedules, medical info, and important contacts.

4

Stay Connected

All guardians receive updates and can coordinate care seamlessly.

Perfect For These Situations

Grandparent Care

Keep grandparents informed about routines, allergies, and emergency contacts when they're watching the kids.

Co-Parenting

Share schedules, school information, and medical updates between households.

Babysitters

Provide temporary access to caregivers with all the information they need.

Emergency Contacts

Ensure trusted friends or family have critical information if you're unavailable.
