Cookie Policy

Last updated: October 01, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Baby Mine uses cookies and similar technologies when you use our website and services.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help websites remember information about your visit.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several purposes:

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Necessary for site functionality (login, security) Session
Performance Cookies Help us understand how visitors use our site 1 year
Functionality Cookies Remember preferences (language, region) 1 year
Targeting Cookies Used for advertising purposes 6 months

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may use cookies from trusted third parties:

  • Google Analytics: Website analytics
  • Facebook Pixel: Advertising measurement
  • Hotjar: User behavior analysis

4. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome
  • Firefox
  • Safari
  • Edge

Note: Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy. The "last updated" date will indicate when changes were made.

6. Contact Us

Questions about our Cookie Policy? Contact us at privacy@babymine.com.

We use cookies to enhance your experience. By continuing to visit this site, you agree to our use of cookies.
