About Baby Mine

Our mission to support parents through every stage of their journey

Our Story

Founded in 2025 by a team of pediatricians, obstetricians, and parenting experts, Baby Mine was created to fill the gaps in modern parenting support. After witnessing countless parents struggling to find reliable, evidence-based information and support, we set out to create a comprehensive platform that would guide families from pregnancy through early childhood.

What began as a small app to track baby's feedings and sleep has grown into a complete parenting ecosystem with tools, resources, and community support for every stage of the journey.

2018 Founded by Mr. Shehan Gunasekara and team
10K First 10,000 users
2025 Launched Baby Mine
100 Families served today

Our Mission & Values

Compassion

We approach every family's journey with empathy and understanding, recognizing that parenting is both joyful and challenging.

Evidence-Based

All our guidance is rooted in current medical research and best practices reviewed by our expert team.

Trust & Safety

We prioritize the security of your family's data and the reliability of our information and services.

Community

We believe in the power of shared experiences and creating spaces where parents can support each other.

Innovation

We continuously evolve our tools and resources to meet the changing needs of modern families.

Inclusivity

We welcome and support all families regardless of structure, background, or parenting approach.
