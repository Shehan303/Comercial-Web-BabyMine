Types of Nanny Services

We offer flexible childcare solutions to fit your family's needs

Full-Time Nannies

Consistent, daily care from a dedicated professional who becomes part of your family.

  • 40+ hours per week
  • Long-term commitment
  • Household management
  • Child development focus

Part-Time Nannies

Regular care on a set schedule that fits with your work or other commitments.

  • 20-30 hours per week
  • Flexible scheduling
  • Before/after school care
  • Weekend availability

On-Demand Care

Last-minute or occasional childcare when you need it most.

  • Date nights
  • Emergency coverage
  • Event childcare
  • Short-notice bookings

Specialized Care

Nannies with expertise in specific areas of childcare.

  • Newborn care specialists
  • Special needs experience
  • Multilingual caregivers
  • Tutoring/nanny hybrids

Our Simple Hiring Process

Find your perfect nanny in just a few easy steps - typically completed within 7-10 days

1

Create Your Family Profile

(5-10 minutes)

Complete our easy questionnaire about your family dynamics, childcare needs, schedule preferences, and any special requirements (like languages spoken or educational background).



Child ages & personalities
Required hours & flexibility
Your priorities (experience, education, etc.)
2

Browse Verified Nanny Profiles

(Browse at your pace)

Access our curated database of pre-screened nannies with complete profiles including:

  • Professional photos and introduction videos
  • Detailed work history and references
  • Certifications (CPR, First Aid, Early Childhood Education)
  • Parent reviews and ratings
  • Availability calendars
Save your favorites to compare candidates side-by-side
3

Schedule Interviews

(1-3 days)

Use our integrated scheduling system to arrange:

Video Interviews

Initial 20-30 minute screening calls through our secure platform

In-Person Meetings

Arrange trial sessions (paid) to see interaction with your children


Pro Tip: Prepare questions about parenting philosophy, discipline approach, and emergency procedures.
4

Comprehensive Background Checks

(24-48 hours processing)

We conduct thorough screenings on your selected candidate including:

National Criminal Check
Sex Offender Registry
Driving Record (if applicable)
Professional Reference Verification
License/Certification Validation
Social Security Trace
All checks meet or exceed industry standards
5

Finalize Your Agreement

(1-2 days)

We provide templates and guidance for:

1. Employment contract review
2. Payroll setup (with tax documentation)
3. Schedule finalization
4. Household orientation
