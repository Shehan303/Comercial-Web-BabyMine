Nanny Services

Our 5-step verification process ensures only the most qualified caregivers:

  1. Identity Verification:
    • Government ID validation
    • Address confirmation
    • Right to work verification
  2. Background Checks:
    • National criminal database search
    • Sex offender registry check
    • Driving record review (if applicable)
  3. Reference Validation:
    • Minimum 3 professional references
    • Personal references upon request
    • Verification calls by our team
  4. Skills Assessment:
    • First aid & CPR certification
    • Child development knowledge test
    • Emergency scenario training
  5. In-Person Interview:
    • Conducted by childcare experts
    • Behavioral assessment
    • Practical skills demonstration
